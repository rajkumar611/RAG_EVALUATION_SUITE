This document provides a consolidated view of ACME Corp's departmental budgets and headcounts for the current fiscal year. All figures are approved and final.

KEY OBSERVATIONS
----------------
Engineering holds the largest share of the total budget at $4.2M and also has the highest cost per employee at $100,000. This reflects the competitive salaries and tooling costs associated with technical roles.

Marketing and Finance share the same cost-per-employee figure of $90,000, though Marketing's overall budget is twice as large due to its higher headcount.

Sales has the lowest cost per employee at $80,000, partially due to a variable commission structure where a portion of compensation is tied to performance rather than fixed salary.

Human Resources is the smallest department by both budget and headcount.
